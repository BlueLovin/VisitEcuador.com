Costa (Coastal Food)

Encebollado (Fish Stew)

This fish based soup is regarded as a national delicacy. It is based with local coast ingredients. If you ask any local ecuadorian, they will tell you that this is their favorite hangover cure, if not their favorite dish period.





Bolon

This is the most famous breakfast from the coastal area of Ecuador. It has three basic ingredients: plantain, cheese, and Ecuador's famous chicharron. It goes great with freshly squeezed orange juice after a long night out on the town.





Pan de Yuca

Ecuadorian pan de yuca are small cheesy breads made with yuca flour. Yuca bread makes a delicious warm appetizer and the breads can be made in advance and baked minutes before serving.





Seco de Pollo

Seco is a popular Ecuadorian meat stew. This delicious meal is always eaten with orange rice.

Mountain Food

Cuy (Guinea Pig)

It may be strange to an american, but this is arguably the most popular meal in Ecuador. Usually accompanied by potatoes and a salad.





Locro

A potato-based soup accompanied by squash, beans, corn, and pumpkin. Pictured above with green onions on top.





llapingachos

These are fried potato pancakes that are usually served with a peanut sauce.
